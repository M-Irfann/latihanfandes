How do I pair these headphones with my device?

To pair the headphones, press and hold the power button for 3 seconds until the LED indicator flashes blue. Then, go to the Bluetooth settings on your device and select "Premium Wireless Headphones" from the available devices list.

Can I use these headphones while they're charging?

Yes, you can use the headphones while they're charging using the included USB-C cable. However, the noise cancellation feature may be slightly less effective during charging.

Are replacement ear pads available?

Yes, replacement ear pads are available for purchase separately. You can find them in the Accessories section of our website or contact our customer service for assistance.

Can I connect to multiple devices simultaneously?

Yes, these headphones support multipoint connection, allowing you to connect to two devices at the same time. This makes it easy to switch between, for example, your laptop and smartphone without re-pairing.
